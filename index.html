Breaking down competitive games
Paul Crumrine

Super Smash Brothers Melee

Super Smash Brothers Melee was originally released in 2001 on the Nintendo Gamecube. However, almost 2 decades later it remains one of the most popular competitive fighting games. The biggest reason for this longevity is the gameplay being near impossible to figure out. In this article we will discuss why that is.

Overwatch

Overwatch is a team based first person shooter with a class system reminiscent of games such as Team Fortress 2. Despite the constant updates to the game, it has been steadily losing steam. In this article we will discuss some of this games failings.
